Limited Coverage

Limited coverage plan provides the basic protection, and may not be adequate. It may cost you less, but the coverage is limited to a pre-defined clause of fixed-amount charges. Read the policy details for full benefit and coverage information.

Comprehensive Coverage

These plans offer more flexible coverage. Most of these plans work like a PPO-style 80-20% plan. This means the insured have to pay 20% of the cost for the first $5,000 per policy period. The policy covers the remaining 80%.
